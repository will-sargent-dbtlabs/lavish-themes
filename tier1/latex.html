The Quiet Architecture of Distinct Themes

A canonical sample for the Lavish theme library

This page renders the same content under every theme in the Lavish library. Use it to pick the look that fits the work, then publish or open accordingly. Each theme is self-contained - one CDN link or one inline stylesheet, no JavaScript, no build step.

1. Introduction

Three things to look at: how the body type feels at long-form length, whether the heading hierarchy reads at a glance, and how inline code, tables, and quotes sit inside prose.

Design is the rendering of intent. Or, less grandly: the choices you make until the page stops fighting you.
