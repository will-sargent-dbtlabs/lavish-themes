This page renders the same content under every theme in the Lavish library. Use it to pick the look that fits the work, then publish or open accordingly. Each theme is self-contained — one stylesheet, no JavaScript, no build step.

Three points of attention

Look at how the body type feels at long-form length. Whether the heading hierarchy reads at a glance. How inline code, tables, and quotes sit inside prose.

On intent

Design is the rendering of intent. Or, less grandly: the choices you make until the page stops fighting you.
