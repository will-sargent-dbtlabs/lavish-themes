This page renders the same comparison content as the rest of the Lavish library, restyled into a SaaS-dashboard look: gradient masthead, card grid, pill badges, gradient table header. Use it for briefs that should feel like a polished internal product page rather than a printed document.

Three points of attention

Look at how the body type feels at long-form length. Whether the heading hierarchy reads at a glance. How inline code, tables, and quotes sit inside cards.

Why this theme exists

Captured from a rendered customer-briefing artifact: orange/purple gradient accents, rounded white cards on a soft gray paper, and pill-shaped status badges. Good for account health summaries, engagement recaps, and anything that benefits from at-a-glance status color.
